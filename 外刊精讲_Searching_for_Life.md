## P4 第三段 · 词汇讲解①

**habitable** /ˈhæbɪtəbl/ （adj. 适合居住的）

- **英英释义：** describes a place or habitat that is suitable for something to live
- **词性：** 形容词
- **用法：** 常用于修饰 place / zone / planet / environment
- **动词形式：** inhabit（居住于）
- **名词形式：** habitat（栖息地） / inhabitant（居民）

**例句：**
1. Is Mars habitable for humans? 火星适合人类居住吗？
2. Scientists are searching for habitable planets in the universe. 科学家正在宇宙中寻找宜居行星。
3. The desert is too dry to be habitable. 沙漠太干燥了，不适合居住。

**反义词：** uninhabitable /ˈʌnɪnhæbɪtəbl/ 不适合居住的
- Without water, this area becomes uninhabitable. 没有水，这片区域就变得无法居住。

---

**zone** /zəʊn/ （n. 区域、地带）

- **英英释义：** an area with a particular feature
- **词性：** 名词
- **用法：** 常搭配特定形容词构成复合名词

**常见搭配：**
- habitable zone 宜居带
- earthquake zone 地震带
- danger zone 危险区
- time zone 时区
- no-parking zone 禁止停车区

**例句：**
1. This city is in an earthquake zone. 这座城市位于地震带。
2. People living in war zones face great danger. 生活在战区的人们面临巨大危险。

**仿写例句：**
> The planet exists in the habitable zone around its sun.
> → 这颗行星位于其太阳周围的宜居带。

## P6 词汇讲解②

**potential** /pəˈtenʃl/ （adj. 潜在的 / n. 潜力）

- **英英释义（adj.）：** describes possibly having qualities or abilities to do something
- **英英释义（n.）：** qualities that allow something to develop in the future
- **词性：** 形容词 / 名词
- **用法：** 作形容词时放在名词前：potential + n.
- 作名词时常用：have potential / show potential

**例句：**
1. This data has potential to help future research. 这些数据有潜力帮助未来的研究。（n. 名词用法）
2. We are looking at the potential risks of this experiment. 我们正在研究这个实验的潜在风险。（adj. 形容词用法）
3. She has great potential as a scientist. 她作为科学家有巨大潜力。

**同义词：** possible, likely, promising

---

**rule out** /ruːl aʊt/ （phrasal v. 排除可能性）

- **英英释义：** dismiss the possibility or idea of something
- **词性：** 短语动词（及物）
- **用法：** rule out + 名词/代词/从句

**例句：**
1. Scientists have ruled out the possibility of life on Venus. 科学家排除了金星存在生命的可能性。
2. We cannot rule out that he is lying. 我们不能排除他在说谎的可能。
3. The police ruled out murder in this case. 警方排除了这起案件的他杀可能。

**反义词：** rule in 列入考虑范围

**仿写例句：**
> They've ruled out that the atmosphere is full of hydrogen.
> → They've ruled out the possibility that Mars could support life.

Neil: Caroline Steele says that scientists are looking at the potential atmosphere around TRAPPIST-1e. She also mentions how scientists have ruled out that TRAPPIST-1e is full of hydrogen or carbon dioxide.

**中文大意：**

## P8 词汇讲解③

**dominated** /ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd/ （adj./v. 占主导地位的，被控制的）

- **英英释义：** controlled by or being the most significant element of something
- **词性：** 动词 dominate 的过去分词，常用 be dominated by
- **用法：** be dominated by + 某人/某物

**例句：**
1. The atmosphere is dominated by nitrogen. 大气层以氮气为主。
2. The tech industry is dominated by a few big companies. 科技行业被少数大公司主导。
3. In this area, agriculture is dominated by rice farming. 这个地区的农业以水稻种植为主。

**名词形式：** domination（主导、支配）
**同义词：** controlled by, ruled by, led by

## P10 词汇讲解④

**fingers crossed** /ˈfɪŋɡəz krɒst/ （习语 祈求好运）

- **英英释义：** an expression we use to wish for hope or luck
- **词性：** 习语 / 固定表达
- **用法：** 可以单独使用，也可以说 keep one's fingers crossed
- **来源：** 最初是基督教手势，后来演变为祈求好运的通用表达

**例句：**
1. Fingers crossed that I pass the exam! 祈祷我能通过考试！
2. We're keeping our fingers crossed for good weather tomorrow. 我们祈求明天有好天气。
3. The interview went well. Now I just have to keep my fingers crossed. 面试很顺利，现在就等好运了。

## P11 重点句型仿写

### 句型①

**原文：** It exists in the habitable zone around its sun.

**结构分析：**
主语 + exist(s) in + the + 形容词 + zone around + 名词

**仿写练习：**
1. The school exists in the safe zone around the city. 这所学校位于城市周边的安全区。
2. Many rare animals exist in the protected zone around the national park. 许多珍稀动物生活在国家公园周边的保护区。

---

### 句型②

**原文：** Scientists have ruled out that it's really full of hydrogen.

**结构分析：**
主语 + have/has ruled out + that + 从句 = 排除了...的可能性

**仿写练习：**
1. The doctor has ruled out that he has a serious disease. 医生排除了他患有严重疾病的可能。
2. Researchers have ruled out that the drug causes side effects. 研究人员排除了这种药物会产生副作用的可能。

---

### 句型③

**原文：** The atmosphere is dominated by nitrogen.

**结构分析：**
主语 + is/are dominated by + 某人/某物 = 以...为主导

**仿写练习：**
1. The class discussion was dominated by a few active students. 课堂讨论由几个活跃的学生主导。
2. The local economy is dominated by tourism. 当地经济以旅游业为主导。
