💬 对话练习 · 咖啡店点单
👩 Barista
"Hey there! Welcome. What can I get started for you?" 🔊
你好呀！欢迎光临，想喝点什么？
👤 You
"I've never been here before. What would you recommend?" 🔊
我第一次来，有什么推荐吗？
👩 Barista
"The oat milk brown sugar latte is super popular!" 🔊
燕麦红糖拿铁最近超火！
👤 You
"Sounds amazing! Can I get that to go?" 🔊
听起来不错！可以打包带走吗？
👩 Barista
"Regular or large?" 🔊
普通还是大杯？
👤 You
"Large. Can I sub the oat milk for soy milk?" 🔊
大杯，能换成豆奶吗？
👩 Barista
"No problem. Half sweet?" 🔊
没问题，半糖？
👤 You
"Yes, that's perfect. What's the total?" 🔊
好的。一共多少钱？
👩 Barista
"We have freshly baked croissants and blueberry muffins." 🔊
我们有刚出炉的牛角包和蓝莓马芬。
👤 You
"I'm tempted, but I'll pass this time." 🔊
有点心动，但这次先不了。
👩 Barista
"That comes to $7.20. Name for the order?" 🔊
一共7.2美元。怎么称呼？
👤 You
"It's Lily. You have a loyalty program?" 🔊
我叫Lily。有会员活动吗？
👩 Barista
"Scan the QR code — buy 10, get 1 free!" 🔊
扫二维码，买10送1！
👤 You
"I'm signing up right now!" 🔊
我现在就注册！

💬 对话练习 · 酒店入住
👤 You
"Hi, I have a reservation under the name Lily." 🔊
你好，我以Lily的名字预订了。
👩 Receptionist
"Welcome! May I see your passport, please?" 🔊
欢迎！请出示您的护照。
👤 You
"Sure. Is breakfast included?" 🔊
给。含早餐吗？
👩 Receptionist
"Yes, 7 to 10 AM. How many nights will you be staying?" 🔊
有的，早7-10点。您住几晚？
👤 You
"Three nights. What time is checkout?" 🔊
三晚。退房是几点？
👩 Receptionist
"11 AM. Could I get a deposit of $100?" 🔊
上午11点。需要100美元押金。
👤 You
"Sure, no problem. Can I get a receipt when I check out?" 🔊
好的，退房时能给我发票吗？
👩 Receptionist
"Of course. Enjoy your stay!" 🔊
当然。祝您入住愉快！

💬 对话练习 · 机场出行
👤 You
"Excuse me, where is the check-in counter for Korean Air?" 🔊
请问，大韩航空的值机柜台在哪？
👩 Staff
"Go straight, then turn left. You'll see it next to Gate 12." 🔊
直走左转，在12号登机口旁边。
👤 You
"Thanks. Can I get a window seat, please?" 🔊
谢谢。可以选靠窗座位吗？
👩 Staff
"Sure. How many suitcases are you checking in?" 🔊
好的。您要托运几件行李？
👤 You
"Just one. Can I carry on this backpack?" 🔊
就一件。这背包能随身带吗？
👩 Staff
"Yes, that's fine. Here's your boarding pass. Your gate will be announced later." 🔊
可以。这是您的登机牌，登机口稍后通知。
👤 You
"What time is boarding?" 🔊
几点登机？
👩 Staff
"Boarding starts at 3:30 PM. Please arrive at the gate 30 minutes early." 🔊
下午3:30开始登机，请提前30分钟到登机口。
