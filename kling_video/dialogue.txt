# 场景字幕对照

## Scene 1 · 进门
Waiter: "Hi, welcome! Two?"
Customer: "Yeah, a table for two, please."
服务员：欢迎光临！两位吗？
顾客：对，两位。

## Scene 2 · 引座扫码
Waiter: "Here's the menu — you can scan the QR code to order."
服务员：这是菜单——扫这个码就能点餐。

## Scene 3 · 扫码
Customer: "Got it. Let me scan it... Alright, it's loading."
顾客：好了，我扫一下……出来了。

## Scene 4 · 看菜单
Friend: "What looks good? I'm starving."
Customer: "The braised beef noodle soup looks good. They've got a lunch combo — noodles plus a drink for 28."
朋友：有什么好吃的？我饿死了。
顾客：红烧牛肉面看着不错。还有午餐套餐——面加饮料28块。

## Scene 5 · 下单
Friend: "Let's both get that. Can we pay here too?"
Customer: "Yeah, WeChat or Alipay. Just hit 'Confirm and Pay.'"
朋友：咱俩都来这个。能直接在这儿付吗？
顾客：能，微信支付宝都行。点'确认并付款'就行。

## Scene 6 · 付款成功
Friend: "Nice. That's it?"
Customer: "Yep. They'll bring it to us."
朋友：好，这就完事了？
顾客：对，他们会送过来。

## Scene 7 · 上菜
Narrator: "And here comes the food. Enjoy your meal!"
旁白：面上来了，请慢用！
