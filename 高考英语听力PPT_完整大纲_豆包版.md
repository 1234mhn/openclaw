## P2 听力全貌

**分值结构：**
- 总分30分，共两节
- 第一节：5题 × 1.5分 = 7.5分（短对话）
- 第二节：15题 × 1.5分 = 22.5分（长对话+独白）

**考试流程：**
- 试音 → 第一节（5段短对话）→ 第二节（5段长对话 + 1-2段独白）

**⚠️ 2026新规：**
- 第一节录音由播放1遍改为播放2遍（浙江等省份已确认执行）
- 总时长保持约20分钟不变
- 策略调整：第一遍抓大意，第二遍补细节+核对

## P3 技巧①：预读抢跑法

**核心口诀：放音前，先抢跑；读选项，反推题**

**三步预读法：**
1. 利用试音 + 每段间隙（10-15秒）快速读选项
2. 名词划异（不同的人/物/地点）、动词划变（不同动作）、数字划准
3. 反向推理：看选项猜问题问什么

**训练要点：**
- 拿到卷子先扫读所有选项，不要等放音
- 每个间隙只预读下一题，不回头看

## P4 真题实战①：预读训练（3道）

**真题1：**
音频来源：2023年新课标I卷听力第1题
题干：What will the speakers do this weekend?
选项：
A. Go to a concert.
B. Go to the beach.
C. Visit their grandparents.
原文：
M: Mom and Dad said they'd take us to the beach this weekend.
W: Great! I can't wait. I'll bring my swimming suit.
M: And don't forget the sunscreen!
答案：B
同义替换：take us to the beach = go to the beach
教学点：先展示选项让学生预判问题类型（问计划），再放音频验证

## P5 技巧②：短对话「三秒定答案」

**核心口诀：短对话节奏快，but之后是重点，听到啥别选啥**

**三大法则：**
1. But法则：前半句是铺垫，but/however/actually后面是答案
2. 否定陷阱：听到直接否定词（not/can't/don't）+ I wish I could, but... 要警惕
3. 同义替换：正确选项一般不直接出现原文原词，而是换个说法

## P6 真题实战②：短对话专练（3道）

**真题1（But法则）：**
音频来源：2024年新课标I卷听力第4题
题干：What does the woman think of the movie?
选项：
A. It's boring.
B. It's interesting.
C. It's too long.
原文：
M: So, what did you think of the movie?
W: Well, the acting was good. Actually, I didn't expect it to be that interesting.
M: Really? I thought it was a bit slow in the middle.
答案：B
解析：Actually引导转折，后面才是真实评价；男生的观点是干扰项

---

**真题2（否定陷阱）：**
音频来源：2023年新课标I卷听力第5题
题干：What will the man do tonight?
选项：
A. Go to a party.
B. Stay at home.
C. Work on his project.
原文：
W: Are you going to Sarah's party tonight?
M: I wish I could, but I haven't finished my history project yet. It's due tomorrow.
W: Oh, that's too bad. Maybe next time.
答案：C
解析：I wish I could, but... = 经典拒绝套路，but后面才是真话

---

**真题3（同义替换）：**
音频来源：2025年新课标I卷听力第6题
题干：How will the woman go to the airport?
选项：
A. By taxi.
B. By subway.
C. By bus.
原文：
M: When's your flight?
W: 7 o'clock tomorrow morning. I need to be there by 5.
M: That's early. Are you taking a cab?
W: No. The subway starts at 5:30, so I'll take the airport express bus instead. It's direct.
答案：C
解析：No否定A，subway时间来不及排除B, airport express bus = by bus

## P7 技巧③：长对话「信号词导航」

**核心口诀：信号词，是路标；听到它们，答案到**

**六大信号词类型：**

| 类型 | 信号词 | 考查方向 |
|------|--------|---------|
| 转折类 | but, however, actually, instead | 真实想法/最终决定 |
| 建议类 | why not, how about, maybe you should | 建议/方案 |
| 因果类 | because, so, therefore, as a result | 原因/结果 |
| 强调类 | especially, above all, the most... | 核心信息 |
| 顺序类 | first, then, finally, after that | 步骤/流程 |
| 态度类 | I'm afraid, unfortunately, to be honest | 否定/遗憾 |

## P8 真题实战③：长对话精讲（3组）

**真题1（度假计划）：**
音频来源：2024年新课标I卷听力第7-8题
题干7：Where does the woman want to go for vacation?
选项：A. A mountain area. B. A coastal city. C. A historical site.
题干8：What is the man mainly worried about?
选项：A. The cost. B. The weather. C. The transportation.
原文：
W: I'm thinking about where to go for our summer vacation.
M: Any ideas so far?
W: I've always wanted to visit that ancient city — Xi'an. You know, the Terracotta Warriors.
M: That would be a historical site! But it's going to be very hot in summer there.
W: We can handle the heat. And I've checked the train tickets — they're quite reasonable actually.
M: I'm not worried about the transportation. The thing is, we just bought a new car, so our budget is a bit tight this year.
W: Don't worry. I've been saving up for this trip.
答案：7-C, 8-A
解析：ancient city + Terracotta Warriors = historical site；The thing is 引出真实顾虑：budget tight = cost

---

**真题2（选课咨询）：**
音频来源：2025年新课标I卷听力第9-10题
题干9：What course does the man suggest the woman take?
选项：A. Creative writing. B. Photography. C. Public speaking.
题干10：What is the woman's concern about the course?
选项：A. It's too time-consuming. B. It's too difficult. C. It's too expensive.
原文：
W: I'm trying to decide which elective course to take this semester.
M: How about photography? I took it last year and it was fantastic.
W: Really? I've heard it requires a lot of equipment.
M: Actually, the school provides cameras and equipment. You just need to show up.
W: That's good to know. But the thing is, it meets three times a week, and I already have a pretty tight schedule.
M: Yeah, it does take up time, but the professor makes it so enjoyable that you won't even notice.
W: Hmm, I'll think about it.
答案：9-B, 10-A
解析：How about = 建议信号词；the thing is 引出顾虑：meets three times a week + tight schedule = time-consuming

---

**真题3（电话改约）：**
音频来源：2023年新课标I卷听力第14-16题
题干14：Why does the woman call the man?
选项：A. To cancel a meeting. B. To change an appointment. C. To confirm a reservation.
题干15：When will the man meet Dr. Smith?
选项：A. On Monday morning. B. On Wednesday afternoon. C. On Friday morning.
题干16：What does the woman ask the man to do?
选项：A. Call back later. B. Bring some documents. C. Send an email.
原文：
W: Hello, Mr. Johnson. This is Dr. Smith's office calling.
M: Yes, speaking.
W: I'm calling to confirm your appointment with Dr. Smith for this Friday morning at 10 o'clock.
M: Oh, actually, something has come up. Could we reschedule it?
W: Let me see. Dr. Smith is available next Monday morning or Wednesday afternoon. Which works for you?
M: Wednesday afternoon would be better. Around 2 o'clock?
W: That's fine. And could you please send us your medical records by email before the appointment?
M: Sure, I'll do that. Over the weekend.
W: Perfect. You're all set for next Wednesday at 2. Have a good day.
答案：14-C, 15-B, 16-C
解析：首句confirm为打电话原因；reschedule引出新的时间；could you please = 请求信号词

## P9 技巧④：四大题型拆解

**核心口诀：先辨题型，再用技巧**

**四大题型：**

| 题型 | 识别特征 | 答题策略 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 数字题 | 选项有数字/时间/价格 | 先写数字，再听关系（单价vs总价） |
| 地点题 | 选项是地点名词 | 锁定场景关键词 |
| 关系题 | 选项是身份关系 | 听称呼+语气+场景词 |
| 主旨题 | 选项是完整句子 | 首句/尾句定主题 |

**高频陷阱：**
- 数字题：注意问的是单价还是总价、是去的时间还是回来的时间
- 地点题：场景词可能交叉出现，锁定最核心的
- 态度题：I'd love to, but... 前面是客气，but后面是真实想法

## P11 技巧⑤：考场时间管理

**三步走策略：**
1. 考前5分钟：抢读所有选项，扫一遍卷子
2. 每段间隙10秒：做完立刻读下一题，绝不回头看
3. 卡住了果断跳：跟住录音比纠结一道题重要10倍

**⚠️ 2026新规应对策略：**
- 第一节改两遍
- 第一遍：整体理解，标记不确定的
- 第二遍：确认答案，补漏
- 不要第一遍就急着选死

**常见失误提醒：**
- 涂卡不及时 → 利用每题间隙涂
- 走神后慌乱 → 果断跳，跟住当前题
- 纠结前一道 → 听力没有回头路

## P13-P15 核心场景精讲：词汇+同义替换

### P13 三大核心场景

**场景① 餐馆：**
核心词汇：order, menu, reserve/reservation, bill/check, waiter/waitress, dish, dessert, tip, treat, go Dutch, on the house
同义替换：
- book a table → make a reservation
- pay the bill → settle the account
- I'd like to invite you → It's my treat / This one's on me
- I'm full → I've had enough / I couldn't eat another bite

**场景② 学校：**
核心词汇：assignment, deadline, exam/quiz, semester, credit, elective/optional, required/compulsory, register/enroll, drop the course, make up exam, lecture, scholarship
同义替换：
- hand in → submit / turn in
- pass the exam → get through / make the grade
- fail → flunk / not make the cut
- difficult → tough / challenging / demanding
- postpone → put off / delay / reschedule

**场景③ 家庭生活：**
核心词汇：do the dishes/laundry, take out the trash, clean the house, grocery shopping, pick up/drop off, babysit, repair/fix, yard/garden, pet
同义替换：
- tidy up → clean up / straighten up
- take care of → look after / babysit
- repair → fix / have it fixed
- go shopping → do the groceries / run errands

### P14 第二梯队场景精讲

**场景④ 交通：**
核心词汇：departure, arrival, boarding gate, check-in, delay, cancel, transfer/layover, luggage, one-way/round trip, platform, fare, traffic jam, rush hour

### P15 核心同义替换速查表

**时间类：**
- 之前 → ahead of time / in advance / beforehand
- 之后 → later on / afterwards
- 立即 → right away / at once / immediately / without delay

**意愿类：**
- 想做 → would like to / feel like / be interested in / be keen on
- 不想 → be reluctant to / not in the mood / rather not

**建议类：**
- Why not... → You might as well... → How about... → It would be a good idea to...

**态度类（⚠️高频陷阱）：**
- I'd love to, but... → 前面客气，but后面真实想法
- I wish I could... → 实际上做不到
- That sounds great, but... → 拒绝前奏

**数字类（⚠️必考）：**
- 15 ↔ 50：靠重音区分 fifTEEN ↔ FIFty
- 16 ↔ 60：sixTEEN ↔ SIXty
