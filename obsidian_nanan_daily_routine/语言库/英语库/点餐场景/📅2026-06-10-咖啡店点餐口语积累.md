# 咖啡店点餐 · 实用口语积累

**📅 时间：** 2026-06-10 14:30
**🏷️ 主题：** 英语-咖啡店点餐场景
**📂 分类：** 语言库/英语库/点餐场景

---

## 📖 重点短语和句式

### 1. 进门打招呼

> **Barista:** "Hey there! Welcome. What can I get started for you?"
> **你：** "I've never been here before. What would you recommend?"

- **What can I get started for you?** — 咖啡店最常用的招呼语，= "想喝什么"
- **What would you recommend?** — 第一次去问推荐，万能句式

### 2. 点单 & 定制

> **你：** "Can I get that to go?"
> **Barista:** "Regular or large?"
> **你：** "Large. Can I sub oat milk for soy milk?"
> **Barista:** "No problem. Half sweet?"

| 句式 | 用法 |
|:----|:------|
| **Can I get...** | 点单万能开头，= "我要..." |
| **to go** | 打包带走（堂食=for here） |
| **sub A for B** | 用B替换A，= swap/replace |
| **Half sweet?** | 半糖（少糖=less sweet，无糖=no sugar） |

### 3. 加单 & 付款

> **Barista:** "We have freshly baked croissants and blueberry muffins."
> **你：** "I'm tempted, but I'll pass this time."
> **Barista:** "That comes to $7.20. Name for the order?"

| 句式 | 用法 |
|:----|:------|
| **I'm tempted** | 有点心动，委婉表达有兴趣 |
| **I'll pass** | 这次算了，婉拒的礼貌说法 |
| **That comes to...** | 共计...元，买单常用 |
| **Name for the order?** | 问怎么称呼，记单用 |

### 4. 会员积分

> **你：** "You have a loyalty program?"
> **Barista:** "Buy 10, get 1 free!"
> **你：** "I'm signing up right now!"

| 句式 | 用法 |
|:----|:------|
| **loyalty program** | 会员积分活动 |
| **Buy X, get Y free** | 买X送Y，促销常用句式 |
| **sign up** | 注册/报名（sign in=登录，sign out=登出） |

---

## ❓ 容易错/混淆的点

- **sub** — 咖啡店/餐厅常用，但正式场合用 **replace/swap** 更合适
- **pass** — 不是"经过"，口语里是"算了/不要了"
- **to go / for here** — 咖啡店必会的一对反义词

### 📺 内容创作者·点餐相关

> **①** "I wanted to mention two things."
> 我想提两件事。——视频开头常用，引出要说的内容

> **②** "If you haven't subscribed to my channel yet, please click the subscribe button below."
> 如果你还没订阅我的频道，请点击下面的订阅按钮。
> 🛠 注意：**subscribe** 要加 **d** → subscribed（haven't + 过去分词）

> **③** "And second, if you're worried I'm going to eat all this food myself — don't worry, my kids are eagerly waiting at home."
> 第二，如果你担心我一个人吃掉所有食物——别担心，我的孩子们正在家里眼巴巴地等着呢。
> 🛠 原句"at number two" → "second"更地道；"i'm not" → "don't worry"更自然

> **④** "I'm going to order something."
> 我要点些东西。——点餐前说的万能句
> 🛠 gonna=going to，口语更常说："I'm gonna order something."

> **⑤** "I would like to order something."
> 我想点些东西。——比"I want"更礼貌的点餐说法
> 🛠 "I'd like to..." = I would like to... 更口语化

> **⑥** "Is it for pickup?"
> 自取吗？——服务员问你怎么取餐
> 🛠 回答："Yes, pickup." / "No, I'll eat here." / "For here."
> 🛠 类似问法："For here or to go?"（堂食还是带走）

> **⑦** "What would you like to order?"（❌ what **do** you like → 是问喜好/习惯）
> 您想点什么？——服务员点餐标准问法
> 🛠 类似说法："Are you ready to order?"（可以点餐了吗）/ "What can I get you?"（给您来点什么）

> **⑧** "Your total comes to + 金额"
> 您一共消费...元。——服务员结账标准说法
> 🛠 例句： "Your total comes to $7.20." / "Your total comes to ¥55."
> 🛠 顾客问："What's the total?" / "How much is it?"
> 🛠 服务员答："That comes to $7.20." / "Your total comes to $7.20."

> **⑨** "For here or to go?" / "Is it for pickup?"
> 堂食还是带走？/ 自取吗？——服务员问怎么取餐
> 🛠 回答： "For here."（堂食）/ "To go."（带走）/ "Pickup."（自取）

> **⑩** "I'll have..." / "I will have..."
> 我要...——点餐最常用的说法之一
> 🛠 比 "I want..." 更礼貌、更地道
> 🛠 类似说法："I'll take..." / "Can I get..."
> 🛠 例句："I'll have a latte." / "I'll have the steak." / "I'll have what she's having."

> **⑪** "Is it for here or to go?"
> 在这吃还是带走？——服务员点餐常用问法
> 🛠 回答： "For here."（堂食）/ "To go."（外带）

> **⑫** "Here you go, the straws are over there."
> 给你，吸管在那边。——服务员给东西时说的话
> 🛠 "Here you go" = "给你"，比"Here it is"更口语化
> 🛠 "over there" = "在那边"，指远处
> 🛠 类似： "Here you are." / "There you go."

> **⑬** "Could I have something?"
> 我能来点东西吗？——礼貌版点餐
> 🛠 "Could" 比 "Can" 更礼貌
> 🛠 类似："Could I have a menu?" / "Could I have some water?"

> **⑭** "I would like a table for four, please."
> 我要一张四人桌。——餐厅入座时的说法
> 🛠 "a table for + 人数" = 几人桌
> 🛠 类似："A table for two, please." / "Do you have a table for three?"
> 🛠 回答： "Right this way."（这边请）/ "Please wait, about 10 minutes."（请稍等约10分钟）

> **⑮** "Here is a table for four."
> 这是四人桌。——服务员带位时说的话
> 🛠 对应问法： "I would like a table for four."
> 🛠 类似： "Right this way."（这边请）/ "Your table is ready."（您的桌位准备好了）

> **⑯** "Can I get you anything to drink?"
> 给您来点什么喝的吗？——服务员问饮品
> 🛠 回答： "I'll have a Coke." / "Just water, please." / "What do you have?"
> 🛠 类似： "What would you like to drink?" / "Any drinks for you?"

> **⑰** "Can I bring you anything else?"
> 还需要别的吗？——服务员上完东西后问是否加单
> 🛠 "anything else" = "还要别的吗"，餐厅最常用
> 🛠 类似："Anything else?"（还要别的吗）/ "Is that all?"（就这些了吗）
> 🛠 回答： "That's all, thanks."（就这些了）/ "Yes, can I have..."（还要...）
