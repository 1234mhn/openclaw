## P3 短对话·考点分析

**短对话10-15秒，只听一遍，信息少但节奏快。**

**五大常考方向：**

| 考点 | 占比 | 典型问法 |
|------|------|---------|
| 时间/日期/数字 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | What time / How much / How long |
| 地点/场景 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Where does it happen |
| 人物关系 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Who are the speakers |
| 动作/计划 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | What will the man do |
| 态度/看法 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | What does she think of |

**致命难点：** 不回头，走神就没了。预读选项是命门。

**解题三步：**
1. 放音前扫选项 → 预判考什么类型
2. 听的时候抓关键词
3. 听到什么不选什么（同义替换才是答案）

## P4 长对话·考点分析

**2-3轮对话，听两遍，每段配2-3题。有完整"剧情"。**

**常考方向：**

| 出题位置 | 考点 | 技巧 |
|---------|------|------|
| 开头第1题 | 主旨大意/话题 | 开头两句定方向 |
| 中间第2题 | 细节信息 | 信号词后是答案 |
| 结尾最后一题 | 推理判断/态度 | 听语气+上下文 |

**长对话的"剧情"套路：**
① 聊一个话题
② 出现转折（but / however / actually）
③ 达成一致或留下悬念

**听两遍分工：**
- 第一遍：听大概 + 排除明显错的选项
- 第二遍：锁定答案

## P5 独白·考点分析

**一段话80-120词，常考题材：**

| 题材 | 典型内容 | 关键词 |
|------|---------|--------|
| 学校通知 | 活动安排、参观须知 | announcement / notice / remember |
| 人物介绍 | 自我介绍、嘉宾介绍 | introduce / welcome / is known for |
| 地点介绍 | 博物馆、景点、学校 | located / famous for / history |
| 生活建议 | 天气、健康、安全 | remember to / don't forget |

**解题关键：**
- 首句定主题
- 顺序词（first / then / finally）对应题目顺序
- 最后一句常考题（建议/要求/提醒）

## P6 高频考点①：数字信息

**数字陷阱只有三种：**

**① -teen vs -ty（最常错）**
fifTEEN（尾巴重音）↔ FIFty（前面重音）
- 告诉学生：重音在哪，答案在哪

**② 单价 vs 总价 vs 找零**
"Each is $15, so two are $30. Here's $50."
- 问单价选15 / 问总价选30 / 问找零选20
- 做题前先看问什么

**③ 时间加减**
"The train leaves at 8:30. We need to be there 30 minutes early."
- 到站时间 = 8:00
- 提前、延迟、增加、减少

**数字专项训练（老师课堂练）：**

练习①：听下面3个句子，写下你听到的数字
1. The ticket costs $18.50.
2. The meeting starts at a quarter to three.
3. She was born on March 15th, 2008.

练习②：判断听到的是 15 还是 50
1. My father is fifty years old.
2. There are fifteen students in the club.
3. The shirt costs fifteen dollars.
4. I have fifty pages left to read.

（答案：50 / 15 / 15 / 50）

## P8 高频考点③：人物关系

**关系题靠两个线索锁定：**

**线索①：称呼方式**
- "Mr. / Mrs. + 姓" → 师生 或 上下级
- 直接叫名字 → 朋友/同学
- "Sir / Madam" → 服务员对顾客

**线索②：场景词锁定**
| 听到 | 关系 |
|------|------|
| Take this medicine / Check your blood pressure | doctor-patient |
| Hand in your homework / Read the text | teacher-student |
| What would you like to order / Can I take your order | waiter-customer |
| How much is this / Can I try this on | shop assistant-customer |

**态度专项训练（老师课堂练）：**

练习①：判断下面语气是积极还是消极
1. That's wonderful! （积极）
2. I'm afraid I can't make it. （消极）
3. It's not as good as I expected. （消极）
4. I'd love to, but I have to finish my homework. （表面积极，实际拒绝）

练习②：下面哪句表达了"拒绝"？
A. I'd love to, but I'm busy. ✅
B. Sure, I'd be happy to.
C. That sounds great! When shall we meet?

## P9 高频考点④：同义替换

**正确选项几乎从不直接出现原文原词——一定换了个说法。**

**五大替换类型：**

**① 动词替换（最常考）**
- buy → purchase / get / pick up
- postpone → put off / delay / reschedule
- hand in → submit / turn in
- recover → get over / be on the mend

**② 形容词/副词替换**
- difficult → tough / challenging / hard
- cheap → reasonable / affordable / a bargain
- busy → tied up / occupied / swamped

**③ 时间替换**
- ahead of time → in advance / beforehand
- right away → immediately / at once / without delay
- later → afterwards / by and by

**④ 因果关系替换**
- because → since / as / the reason is
- so → therefore / as a result / thus

**⑤ 建议类替换**
- Why not → How about / You might as well / It would be a good idea to

## P10 题型专项训练① · 短对话

**训练目标：3秒预判+抓住关键词**

🎵 音频：长沙市近3年中考真题

**第一题（地点类）：**
选项：
A. In a restaurant
B. In a hospital
C. In a library

原文：
W: Good morning. What seems to be the problem?
M: I've been coughing for three days and have a sore throat.
W: Let me take your temperature first.

答案：B（关键词：coughing / sore throat / take temperature = 医院）

**第二题（数字类）：**
选项：
A. $8
B. $10
C. $16

原文：
M: How much is the ticket?
W: $8 for adults and half price for children.
M: So I need two adult tickets and one child ticket.

答案：C（$8×2 + $4 = $20… 问的是？注意看题干问什么）

**第三题（态度类）：**
选项：
A. Interesting
B. Boring
C. Too long

原文：
W: What did you think of the movie?
M: I thought it was a bit slow at the beginning, but the ending was really exciting.

答案：A（but后面的才是真实想法：ending was exciting）

M: When's your flight?
W: 7 o'clock tomorrow morning. I need to be there by 5.
M: That's early. Are you taking a cab?
W: No. The subway starts at 5:30, so I'll take the airport express bus.

答案：C（No 否定A, subway 来不及排除B, airport express bus = by bus）

**交通真题②：**
题干：Why is the man late?
选项：A. He missed the bus B. His car broke down C. He got stuck in traffic

原文：
W: You're late again. What happened?
M: Sorry. There was an accident on the highway, so traffic was backed up for miles.
W: You should have taken the side roads.

答案：C（traffic was backed up = stuck in traffic）

**天气真题①：**
题干：What's the weather like tomorrow?
选项：A. Sunny B. Rainy C. Cloudy

原文：
W: Did you check the weather for tomorrow?
M: Yes. The rain will stop tonight, and tomorrow will be partly cloudy but no rain.
W: Good enough. Let's still go hiking.

答案：C（partly cloudy but no rain = cloudy）

**天气真题②：**
题干：What does the woman suggest they do?
选项：A. Stay at home B. Go to the beach C. Bring an umbrella

原文：
M: Look at the dark clouds outside!
W: Yeah, it looks like it's going to pour any minute.
M: We were going to walk to the restaurant.
W: We'd better bring umbrellas. Or we could just order delivery instead.

答案：C（bring umbrellas = bring an umbrella）

M: Hi, I'd like to book a table for four at 7 pm tonight.
W: Let me check... Yes, we have a table available.

答案：A（book a table = make a reservation）

W: Could I leave a message?
M: Sure. I'll make sure he gets it.

答案：B（Could I leave a message = 留言）

**三件事，每天10分钟：**
