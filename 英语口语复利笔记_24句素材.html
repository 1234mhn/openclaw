场景式口语复利笔记
⑟ My eyes are still heavy. They feel glued shut.
眼睛还很重像粘住了一样。
📍 单词
heavy 沉重的
glued shut 粘住一样
barely 勉强
eyelid 眼皮
💡 多种表达
• I'm so sleepy I can barely open my eyes.
• My eyelids feel like they weigh a ton.
• I can't keep my eyes open.
⚠️ 中式避雷
❌ I'm very tired in my eyes.
✅ My eyes are still heavy.
heavy比tired更形象
🔧 万能句型
[body part] feel(s) [adj] — My legs feel weak.
✍️ 三层改写
① 基础 I can't open my eyes.
② 进阶 My eyes are so heavy they feel glued shut.
③ 个人化 My eyes always feel glued shut in winter.
🎭 实景对话
A: You awake?
B: Barely. My eyes feel glued shut.
✍️ 反向仿写
中文：早上眼睛像粘住了一样睁不开。
参考：My eyes feel glued shut every morning.
